References & Evidence Base

The Strong Steps toolkit is built on current physiotherapy research, clinical best practices, and the lived experiences of patients navigating ankle recovery.

Our Approach

  • Graded Exposure: Evidence consistently shows that gradually exposing healing tissues to load leads to stronger ligaments and less fear of reinjury compared to prolonged rest.
  • Neuromuscular Control: Our Phase 3 balance exercises are based on research demonstrating that proprioceptive training significantly reduces the risk of recurrent ankle sprains.
  • Patient Education: Understanding the mechanics of injury and normal healing timelines improves adherence to home exercise programs and reduces anxiety (kinesiophobia).

Guiding Principles

The information provided incorporates principles from leading sports medicine guidelines, including the PEARL (Protection, Elevation, Avoid Anti-inflammatories, Rest, Load) consensus statements and the Ottawa Ankle Rules for fracture screening.
