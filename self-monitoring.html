Normal Discomfort vs. Warning Signs

Normal Healing Discomfort ✔️

  • Mild aching after exercises
  • Stiffness in the morning that improves as you move
  • Pain rating around 2-4 out of 10 during rehab
  • Symptoms that settle down within 24 hours after activity

Warning Signs ❌

  • Sharp, stabbing pain that forces you to limp heavily
  • Sudden increase in swelling after weeks of it going down
  • Pain that keeps you awake at night
  • Feeling of the ankle giving way completely

If you experience warning signs, stop exercises, rest, and consider seeking professional medical advice.
